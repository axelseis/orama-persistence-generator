# Organic Minimalism

## Definition
A warm evolution of minimalism that prioritizes calm, clarity, and natural materials. It uses earthy palettes, humanist typography, and subtle textures that evoke paper, clay, wood, or sand.

## Intent and use
- Create a calm, trustworthy, and nature-connected experience.
- Balance simplicity with warmth and tactility.
- Ideal for wellness, sustainability, healthcare, education, and lifestyle brands.

## Color palettes (examples)
- **Earthy Calm**: #F3EFE6 (linen), #D8CBB8 (sand), #B7A58A (taupe), #7E8A6A (olive), #3E3E3E (text)
- **Soft Terracotta**: #F6E2D4 (cream), #E2B9A3 (terracotta), #C3C7B0 (sage), #9FB7C0 (dusty blue), #2F2F2F (text)
- **Natural Paper**: #F7F2E9 (paper), #E0D6C8 (kraft), #C7B59D (clay), #A2B0A1 (moss), #1F1F1F (ink)

## Design rules
- **Layout**: generous whitespace, low density, clear hierarchy.
- **Textures**: subtle grain or paper texture; never overpowering.
- **Shapes**: soft edges, organic geometry, minimal ornament.
- **Color**: muted, low-saturation tones; avoid neon or harsh contrast.
- **Motion**: slow, gentle micro-animations; avoid sharp bounces.

## Typography (fonts available in Penpot)
Use humanist or soft sans for readability and warmth.
- **Headings**: "Sora", "Manrope", "Merriweather Sans", "Nunito"
- **Body**: "Public Sans", "Noto Sans", "Inter", "Merriweather Sans"
- **Optional serif accent**: "Spectral", "Lora"

## Layout and components
- **Cards** with subtle borders or soft shadows.
- **Buttons** rounded corners, soft fill, minimal stroke.
- **Inputs** with light borders and calm focus states.
- **Icons** simple, rounded, low-detail line icons.

## Illustration and imagery
- Botanical line art, abstract organic shapes.
- Natural photography with warm, low-contrast lighting.
- Minimal gradients inspired by sky, sand, and clay.

## Accessibility
- Keep contrast sufficient on muted palettes.
- Use clear type sizes and line-height for readability.
- Avoid low-contrast text on textured backgrounds.

## Agent keywords
organic, warm, earthy, calm, minimal, natural, paper, soft, humanist, whitespace
