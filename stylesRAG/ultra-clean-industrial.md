# Ultra-Clean Industrial

## Definition
A minimal, technical style built on precise geometry, neutral palettes, and efficient typography. It communicates clarity, trust, and functional precision.

## Intent and use
- Signal reliability, rigor, and scalability.
- Ideal for SaaS, tech products, medical, and premium industrial branding.
- Best when content needs to feel clean and systematic.

## Color palettes (examples)
- **Cold Precision**: #F5F6F7 (off-white), #D9DDE2 (cool gray), #AEB7C2 (steel), #2F3A45 (slate), #0E1114 (text)
- **Minimal Tech**: #FFFFFF (white), #E6E9ED (light gray), #B9C2CC (mid gray), #3A4754 (charcoal), #1A1F24 (text)
- **Clinical Neutral**: #F8F9FB (paper), #E1E6EC (mist), #C0CBD6 (cool blue-gray), #5B6772 (graphite), #111417 (text)

## Design rules
- **Layout**: strict grid, consistent spacing, aligned edges.
- **Shapes**: sharp corners or small radii; avoid soft blobs.
- **Density**: low to medium; use whitespace as structure.
- **Hierarchy**: clear typographic scale, no decorative noise.
- **Motion**: minimal, functional, fast transitions.

## Typography (fonts available in Penpot)
Use clean, modern sans families with multiple weights.
- **Headings**: "Inter", "IBM Plex Sans", "Plus Jakarta Sans", "Manrope", "Sora"
- **Body**: same family in regular or medium
- **UI**: same family in medium or semibold

## Layout and components
- **Cards** with light borders, no heavy shadows.
- **Buttons** simple, rectangular, subtle hover.
- **Tables** with clear alignment and row spacing.
- **Icons** thin stroke, geometric.

## Illustration and imagery
- Minimal vector icons and line diagrams.
- Product photos with clean backgrounds.
- Avoid heavy textures or grain.

## Accessibility
- High contrast for text and UI states.
- Large base font size and consistent spacing.
- Avoid light weights for body text.

## Agent keywords
minimal, precise, grid, clean, technical, neutral, structured, reliable, crisp
