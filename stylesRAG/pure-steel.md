# Pure Steel

## Definition
A futuristic metallic style built on chrome-like finishes, cold grays, and sharp contrast. It suggests high-tech precision, durability, and premium engineering.

## Intent and use
- Communicate innovation, performance, and technical confidence.
- Ideal for hardware, automotive, fintech, and luxury tech brands.
- Works well for dark mode and high-contrast UI.

## Color palettes (examples)
- **Chrome Core**: #F2F4F7 (light steel), #C8D0D8 (silver), #8A96A1 (gunmetal), #2B323A (graphite), #0B0F14 (text)
- **Neon Steel**: #EDEFF2 (steel), #9BA7B3 (cool gray), #2A323B (dark), #00E5FF (neon cyan), #C400FF (neon violet)
- **Industrial Night**: #0E1216 (black), #1C232B (charcoal), #4A5662 (metal), #9AA3AC (steel), #E8EDF2 (light)

## Design rules
- **Surfaces**: glossy, reflective, or brushed metal textures.
- **Contrast**: strong highlights and deep shadows to imply depth.
- **Geometry**: sharp edges, angled shapes, mechanical symmetry.
- **Accents**: neon or electric highlights used sparingly.

## Typography (fonts available in Penpot)
Choose condensed or technical sans and mono accents.
- **Headings**: "IBM Plex Sans", "Anton", "Sora", "Plus Jakarta Sans"
- **Body**: "Inter", "IBM Plex Sans", "Public Sans"
- **Mono accents**: "IBM Plex Mono", "Space Mono"

## Layout and components
- **Panels** with metallic borders and subtle gradients.
- **Buttons** with crisp outlines and glow accents.
- **Badges** with pill shapes and hard highlights.
- **Graphs** with high-contrast lines and gridlines.

## Illustration and imagery
- 3D metallic renders, product close-ups, industrial surfaces.
- Abstract technical grids and microchip-like patterns.

## Accessibility
- Ensure neon accents do not reduce readability.
- Maintain sufficient text contrast in dark mode.
- Avoid thin strokes on dark backgrounds.

## Agent keywords
metallic, chrome, steel, futuristic, sharp, neon, technical, high-contrast, durable
