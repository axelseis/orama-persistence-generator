# Neobrutalism

## Definition
A bold, raw style that favors high contrast, oversized type, and hard edges. It embraces deliberate friction and visual impact while keeping structure readable.

## Intent and use
- Create strong memorability and attitude.
- Ideal for creative portfolios, events, youth brands, and rebellious products.

## Color palettes (examples)
- **Brutal Contrast**: #FFFFFF (white), #000000 (black), #FF3B30 (red), #00C2FF (cyan), #FFD400 (yellow)
- **Hard Primary**: #F7F7F7 (paper), #1C1C1C (black), #FF6B6B (red), #4D7CFE (blue), #F5B700 (yellow)
- **Acid Pop**: #F9F9F9 (white), #111111 (black), #00FF90 (green), #FF4DFF (magenta), #FFB300 (orange)

## Design rules
- **Type scale**: oversized headlines, stacked lines, tight leading.
- **Contrast**: strong color blocks and hard edges.
- **Layout**: simple grid but with intentional tension and asymmetry.
- **UI**: thick borders, sharp corners, minimal shading.

## Typography (fonts available in Penpot)
Use bold display for impact and clean sans for readability.
- **Headlines**: "Anton", "League Spartan", "Archivo Black", "Black Ops One", "Bungee", "Bebas Neue"
- **Subheads**: "Inter", "IBM Plex Sans", "Plus Jakarta Sans"
- **Body**: "Work Sans", "Public Sans"

## Layout and components
- **Cards** with thick borders and no shadow.
- **Buttons** with hard edges and high-contrast fills.
- **Labels** in uppercase, tight tracking.

## Illustration and imagery
- Flat, high-contrast shapes.
- Collage-like cutouts and poster-style imagery.

## Accessibility
- Keep body text in legible sans.
- Avoid high-contrast flashing patterns.

## Agent keywords
brutal, bold, contrast, oversized, raw, sharp, poster, high-impact
