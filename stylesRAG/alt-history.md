# Alt-History

## Definition
A remix of vintage motifs with modern interventions. It blends archival typography and imagery with contemporary contrasts like neon accents, modern grids, or bold vector overlays.

## Intent and use
- Combine heritage and modernity in one brand voice.
- Ideal for cultural events, editorial branding, and premium creative products.

## Color palettes (examples)
- **Archive Neon**: #F3EBDD (paper), #2C2C2C (ink), #FF3B30 (red), #00D1FF (cyan), #8B6B5A (sepia)
- **Retro Modern**: #F5F0E6 (cream), #3C3C3C (charcoal), #6E7A6D (sage), #C35B4B (rust), #F2C94C (accent)
- **Classic Contrast**: #F1E9DC (paper), #1E1E1E (black), #7C6A58 (brown), #6A7DBB (blue), #C87D4A (orange)

## Design rules
- **Mix eras**: pair classic typography with modern sans or monos.
- **Overlays**: use modern geometric shapes over archival imagery.
- **Contrast**: bold color accents on muted vintage bases.
- **Hierarchy**: clean modern structure with vintage ornamentation.

## Typography (fonts available in Penpot)
Combine classic serif display with clean modern sans.
- **Headings**: "Cinzel", "Playfair Display", "EB Garamond"
- **Subheads / UI**: "Inter", "IBM Plex Sans", "Work Sans"
- **Body**: "Spectral", "Merriweather", "Newsreader"

## Layout and components
- **Posters** with layered typography and mixed imagery.
- **Cards** with archival textures and bold modern tags.
- **Badges** mixing seals with neon labels.

## Illustration and imagery
- Archival photos, etchings, or vintage ads.
- Modern vector overlays or gradient blocks.

## Accessibility
- Keep body text in a clean serif or sans.
- Avoid high-contrast overlays behind small text.

## Agent keywords
vintage remix, archival, modern contrast, neon accent, retro, hybrid, heritage-modern
