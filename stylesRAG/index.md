# Styles RAG Index

This index groups the styles detected in the reports by families. Each style will have its own file with definition, palettes, rules, and fonts available in Penpot.

## 1) Sensory / Tactile / Human

- **Tactile Maximalism / Squishy-uishy UI**: Volumetric, soft interfaces with textures, bounce, and digital physicality.
- **Imperfect by Design / Human Touch**: Deliberate imperfections, hand-drawn strokes, collage, and asymmetry for authenticity.
- **Anti-AI Crafting**: Analog materiality, human flaws, and traditional techniques as proof of authenticity.

## 2) Natural / Organic / Warm

- **Organic Minimalism**: Warm minimalism with earthy palettes, humanist typography, and subtle natural micro-textures.

## 3) Industrial / Tech / Metallic

- **Ultra-Clean Industrial**: Pure geometry, cool/neutral palettes, and efficient type; functional precision.
- **Pure Steel**: Realistic metallic finishes, cool grays, and neon accents; technical futurism.

## 4) Vintage / Heritage / Editorial

- **Apothecary Aesthetic**: Vintage botanical look, serif type, rigid grids; natural and clinical-craft tone.
- **Heritage Etch**: Fine engraving, hatching and cross-hatching; tradition and premium authority.
- **Alt-History**: Vintage motifs remixed with modern accents (neon/vector); temporal contrast.
- **Imprinted**: Visible print textures and manual marks; craftsmanship and sustainability.
- **Narrative Pop**: Editorial density, layered typography, and explicit visual storytelling.

## 5) Excess / Contrast / Expressive

- **New Maximalism**: Abundant color, patterns, and visual density with narrative intent.
- **Minimalist Maximalism**: Minimal base with maximal accents (giant type, overlays, controlled drama).
- **Neobrutalism**: Raw layouts, massive type, high contrast, and deliberate friction.

## 6) Translucent / Depth / Premium Digital

- **Glassmorphism**: Translucent layers, blur, and vivid gradients for depth and elegant hierarchy.

## 7) Typography in Motion

- **Kinetic / Expressive Typography**: Text as the primary visual element in motion, distortion, and impact.

## 8) Systems / Experience / Personalization

- **Multi-Device UX / Fluid Continuity**: Cross-device coherence, task continuity, and adaptive design.
- **AI-Driven Design / Extreme Personalization**: Dynamic interfaces that adapt to user and context.
