# Minimalist Maximalism

## Definition
A hybrid style: a clean, minimal base with bold maximal accents. It uses generous whitespace and strict structure, then injects drama with oversized type, strong color, or expressive visuals.

## Intent and use
- Combine clarity with personality.
- Ideal for modern luxury, editorial sites, and premium digital products.

## Color palettes (examples)
- **Clean Drama**: #FFFFFF (white), #EDEFF2 (light gray), #1F1F1F (text), #FF4D4F (accent red), #3D6DFF (accent blue)
- **Soft Base + Pop**: #F7F2E9 (paper), #DAD6CF (warm gray), #2A2A2A (text), #FFB800 (accent), #7B61FF (accent)
- **Neutral + Neon**: #F5F5F5 (light), #C9CFD6 (gray), #111111 (text), #00D1FF (neon), #C400FF (neon)

## Design rules
- **Base**: minimal structure, strong grid, generous spacing.
- **Accents**: one or two bold elements per screen.
- **Type**: oversized headline paired with clean body copy.
- **Color**: mostly neutral with concentrated accent areas.

## Typography (fonts available in Penpot)
Clean sans base plus expressive display accents.
- **Headlines**: "Playfair Display", "DM Serif Display", "Bungee", "League Spartan"
- **Body**: "Inter", "Plus Jakarta Sans", "IBM Plex Sans", "Public Sans"
- **Accents**: "Nabla", "Rubik Glitch" (short only)

## Layout and components
- **Hero** with big headline and sparse supporting text.
- **Cards** simple with one bold accent color.
- **Buttons** minimal with a single standout color.

## Illustration and imagery
- Minimal images with one dramatic focal point.
- Large negative space with a bold graphic element.

## Accessibility
- Do not overuse display fonts.
- Keep body text in high-contrast, clean sans.

## Agent keywords
minimal base, bold accent, contrast, whitespace, hybrid, luxury, clean drama
