# New Maximalism

## Definition
A vivid, expressive style built on abundance: layered patterns, saturated color, and dense visual information. It is complex by design, but still intentional.

## Intent and use
- Create strong personality and visual richness.
- Ideal for fashion, music, culture, and bold creative brands.

## Color palettes (examples)
- **Color Riot**: #FF5A5F (red), #00C2FF (cyan), #FFD400 (yellow), #7B61FF (violet), #111111 (black)
- **Neon Garden**: #00FFB2 (green), #FF4DFF (magenta), #FFB800 (yellow), #4D7CFE (blue), #F7F7F7 (paper)
- **Festival Mix**: #FF8A00 (orange), #F53D68 (pink), #3EE6A0 (mint), #5A5CFF (purple), #1F1F1F (text)

## Design rules
- **Layers**: multiple overlapping elements and textures.
- **Patterns**: bold repeats, stripes, dots, and gradients.
- **Type**: big, expressive headlines with contrasting body text.
- **Hierarchy**: clear anchors to avoid chaos.

## Typography (fonts available in Penpot)
Strong display with a stable body font.
- **Headlines**: "Bungee", "Bungee Inline", "Nabla", "Rubik Glitch", "Tilt Neon", "Tilt Prism"
- **Subheads**: "League Spartan", "Archivo"
- **Body**: "Inter", "IBM Plex Sans", "Public Sans", "Work Sans"

## Layout and components
- **Hero** with oversized type and layered visuals.
- **Cards** with bold borders and patterns.
- **Badges** as stickers or stamps.

## Illustration and imagery
- Collage, bold vector shapes, and mixed media textures.
- High-saturation photography with overlays.

## Accessibility
- Keep body text clean and readable.
- Use display fonts only for short words.

## Agent keywords
maximal, layered, saturated, bold, expressive, dense, pattern, vibrant
